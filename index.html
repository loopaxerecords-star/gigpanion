App Manual

1. Core Setup

  • Microphone: Required for Tuner.
  • Keep Awake: Toggle in Settings to prevent screen dimming during a set.
  • Stage Timer: Tap the clock in the top bar to start/pause your set runtime countdown.

2. Performance

  • Navigation: Swipe, use arrows, or select from the top dropdown.
  • Transpose: Tap + or - on the song slide to instantly shift keys.
  • Scroll: Auto-scroll lyrics based on tempo.

3. Setlist Management

  • Profiles: Save different setlists for different gigs in the Setlist Manager.
  • ChordPro Support: Import .pro/.txt files! We automatically parse chords and display them beautifully in line with lyrics.
  • Advanced Chords: Type complex chords like Cmaj7, Em9, D/F#, or Gsus4. They are generated dynamically!
  • Reorder & Export: Drag and drop your order or export JSON backups.

4. Pro Tips

  • Metronome Flash: Tap the lightning bolt icon in the metronome controls to flash the screen on the downbeat. Great for dark stages!
  • Drummer Mode: Locks down the screen and displays a giant drum icon. Ideal for preventing accidental touches while playing.
  • Chord Preview: Tap any chord block in the UI to hear a synthesized preview of exactly how it sounds.
